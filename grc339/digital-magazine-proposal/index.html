Introduction

Bound Book Club

Welcome to Bound — a beginner friendly book club for new and returning readers to discover books they’ll actually enjoy. Bound features social media's favorite picks, honest reviews, and trending authors that make reading feel fun, exciting, and easy to get into. Whether you’re starting your very first reading journey or trying to rediscover a forgotten hobby, we’re here to help you find stories worth diving into!
