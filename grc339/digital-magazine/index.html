Emily Henry Takeover

Emily Henry is a bestselling author known for witty, emotionally layered contemporary romances that blend sharp humor with heartfelt explorations of love, grief, and personal growth. Her novels, including Beach Read and Book Lovers, often feature complex characters who challenge each other in unexpected ways while discovering what they truly want in life and relationships.

Current Fan Favorites

Keep your reading spark alive with these picks! Whether you’re searching for your next obsession or simply want to see what’s trending, these popular picks are guaranteed to keep you turning pages.

Into the Blue

Into the Blue is a sweeping coming-of-age story set against the backdrop of the 1970s music scene, where a young singer-songwriter’s rising fame becomes entangled with love, ambition, and loss. As her career takes off, she must navigate the costs of success while confronting the relationships and choices that shape her identity.

Intermezzo

Sally Rooney’s Intermezzo is an emotionally rich novel exploring grief, family, love, and the complexities of human connection through the lives of two brothers after the death of their father. With Rooney’s signature sharp dialogue and intimate storytelling, the novel offers a deeply personal look at relationships, vulnerability, and identity.

The Silent Patient

Alex Michaelides’ The Silent Patient is a gripping psychological thriller that follows Alicia Berenson, a famous painter who mysteriously stops speaking after being accused of murdering her husband. Packed with suspense, shocking twists, and dark secrets, the novel keeps readers guessing until its unforgettable ending.

Never Lie

Never Lie is a gripping psychological thriller about a newlywed couple trapped in a remote mansion during a snowstorm, where they uncover chilling secrets tied to the home’s former owner. As mysterious tapes reveal disturbing truths, shocking twists keep readers guessing until the very end.

It Ends With Us

It Ends with Us follows Lily Bloom as she navigates a complicated relationship that forces her to confront painful patterns from her past while trying to build a better future. The story explores love, resilience, and the difficulty of breaking cycles of abuse, culminating in emotionally charged choices that redefine what strength really means.

Beach Read

Beach Read follows two writers with opposite styles and unresolved history who end up living in neighboring beach houses for the summer, where a writing challenge forces them to swap genres. As they push each other creatively, they uncover deeper truths about love, grief, and what they really want from their stories and their lives.
